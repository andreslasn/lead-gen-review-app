Transzparencia

A Zentiva Pharma Kft. a Generikus Gyógyszergyártók és Forgalmazók Magyarországi Érdekvédelmi Egyesületének tagjaként magára nézve kötelezőnek ismeri el a Generikus Transzparencia Kódexet, illetve a Gyógyszer-Kommunikációs Etikai Kódexet és a benne foglaltaknak megfelelően kíván eleget tenni kötelességének az etikus gyógyszeripari működés érdekében.

HCPs Fees

Source: Code of Conduct, Section 7.1, page 14

Includes contracted amount for fees for services.
Does not include expenses such as travel, accommodation, and meals.
Does not include fees for R&D or market research.

 

HCP O2 Meetings, Site Visits

Source: Code of Conduct, Section 7.1, Option 2, page 15

For company organized meetings (section 4.3), third party organized congresses (section 4.4), and site visits (section 4.5), include travel, accommodation, meals, registration fees, etc.

HCO

Source: Code of Conduct, Section 7.1, page 15

Fee for service and consultancy:
1. Includes contracted amounts for services.
2. Does not include amounts for meals, travel, and accommodation.
3. Does not include amounts for R&D or market research activities.

PO

Source: Code of Conduct, Section 7.1, page 14

Fee for service includes amount provided under contract for each patient organization.
